Beginning the Gospel Message

THE GOSPEL MESSAGE

  1. GOOD NEWS

The Bible says that God created us, and He loves each of us—every person on earth.
(For the Lord is Good and His love endures forever, His faithfulness continues through all generations)

Because of His love for us, God has provided a way for each person to live forever with Him.
Anyone can have this everlasting life with God, but there is a problem that stands in the way— it's like a barrier or wall.

  1. THE BARRIER

A. Man's Sin

The problem is we have all sinned and face separation from God.

Sin is anything we do (1 John 3:4), say (Matthew 12:37), or think (Proverbs 24:9a) that is against God's law.
It is also not doing what we should do (James 4:17).

"We have all sinned and fall short of God's glory (Romans 3:23)"

God's standard is perfection—living without sin.
God cannot tolerate evil (Habakkuk 1:13).

B. Punishment for sin

The punishment for sin is death and separation from God (Romans 6:23; Isaiah 59:2).

Even our good deeds are like dirty rags (Isaiah 64:6).
God is holy and just and does not leave sin unpunished (Exodus 34:7).

So where is the hope?
The hope is found in God Himself.
