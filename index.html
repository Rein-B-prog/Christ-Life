Deliverance Church is located in Machakos town Kariobangi behind Mali Kiti market. It serves the community in shaping religious activities and nurturing young people's talents in ministry.

The ministry is open to all who seek God. Under the grace our father Dr Bishop Gedion Mbithi

We train on soul winning campaigning (Mark 16:15).
This platform helps young believers grow in Christ Jesus and also non-believers to be won into the Kingdom of God.
