3. God

  1. Truth about God

God wants to be known. God desires that we know Him. Jeremiah 29:13 says, "You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart." God reveals Himself through creation, His Word (the Bible), and the Holy Spirit.

God makes it possible for human beings to have a personal relationship with Him as our Father (Romans 1:19–20; Psalm 139).

  • "It's important to know the truth about God."
    As we learn truths about God, we are set free from darkness.
  • "God is holy and just"
    Because He is holy and just, He must punish sin. He is fair and will judge each person.
  • "God is also loving"
    God loves us with an everlasting love (Jeremiah 31:3).
    He provided a way that satisfies both His justice and His love.

4. GOD'S SOLUTION – THE CHRIST

  1. God Has Provided
  • God provided someone without sin to pay for our sins.
  • His name is Jesus Christ
  • Jesus is fully God and fully man.
  1. Jesus' Complete Payment
  • Jesus lived a perfect life and died for our sins. "The Lord laid on Him the iniquity of us all" (Isaiah 53:6).
  1. Transferring the Burden of Sin

When Jesus was crucified, two criminals were with Him. One mocked Him, but the other believed.

When the believing criminal trusted Jesus, his sins were forgiven. He received peace with God and eternal life.

Jesus' last words were "It is finished" — meaning the payment for sin was complete.

Those who accept Jesus are forgiven and restored to God.
