Scripture

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."

— Psalm 119:105


John 6:63

"It is the Spirit who gives life; the flesh is no help at all.
The words that I have spoken to you are spirit and life."

Hebrews 11:6

"Without faith it is impossible to please him, for whoever would draw near to God
must believe that he exists and that he rewards those who seek him."

Our Response

God tells us in the Bible how we can receive this gift:

  1. Faith

    We must be willing to let go of everything else we trust in and place our full faith in Jesus Christ. Salvation is not by works, but by grace through faith (Ephesians 2:8–9).

  2. Repent

    To repent means to be sorry for sin and turn to God, allowing Him to guide and change your life.

  3. Believe

    Believe means fully entrusting your life to Christ as Savior and Lord. It is more than knowing God exists—it is total commitment.

  4. Receive – Prayer of Salvation

    You receive salvation by speaking to God in prayer and accepting His gift.

    Suggested Prayer:

    "Dear God, I know I am a sinner. I repent of my sins. I believe You died for me. I place my trust in You as my Savior and Lord. Please forgive me and give me everlasting life. Help me follow You all my life. Amen."

    Salvation brings peace with God (Luke 15:10).
