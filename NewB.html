New Believer

All believers in Christ have everlasting life beginning at the point of salvation. The Bible says anyone who believes in Him will not perish but have everlasting life (John 3:16). It also says, "I give them eternal life, and they shall never perish; no one can snatch them out of my hand." (John 10:28)

Growing as a Christian

Below are 5 important things you can do to grow in Christ:

  1. Prayer

    Stay in communication with God daily. Bring your thanksgiving and requests to Him. (Philippians 4:6)

  2. Read God's Word

    The Bible is God's word. Read it daily and use it as your guide for life.

  3. Worship God

    Worship means honoring and praising God for who He is. (Psalm 95:6–7)

  4. Follow God

    Obey God's word and live according to His will with gratitude.

  5. Fellowship with other Christians

    Spend time with other believers to grow and stay strong in faith. (Hebrews 10:24–25)
